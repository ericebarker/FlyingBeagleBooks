Flying Beagle Books, Saxapahaw, NC


Flying Beagle Books is a second-hand bookseller in the artistic, eclectic, creative community of Saxapahaw, North Carolina. We offer fun and informative books available online from our village for global thinkers. Our brick and mortar may be closed, but you can still find us in the village and meet Roswell The Flying Beagle Himself. We are a local 'Mom & Pop' business and truly love our ties to the community and we are always happy to make new friends. See you on Saturdays in Saxapahaw!

Welcome to Saxapahaw!


Here on the rural banks of the Haw River, is the community of Saxapahaw. We may be a small village, but we are full of big ideas. Our local five star gas station has some of the best food you'll find in North Carolina, and our Euro style gastro-pub will amaze you with local brews and pub fare. The Haw River Ballroom and Cup 22 coffee house are perfect for lounging during the day and great music at night. The Haw River Ballroom is also a perfect wedding venue, with your perfect meal catered by the Sax Gen Store! Right at the crossroads by the river you will find the Haw River Canoe and Kayak Company who would love to take you for a paddle down the Haw. Haw River Farmhouse Ales is now open, producing some of the finest craft beers available in the state, and their tasting room is almost always open. Left Bank Butchery is our local whole-animal butcher, who specializes in meat from local farms and old-world style cuts not available at your chain groceries.

Don't forget, every 1st Wednesday of the month at The Eddy Pub in Saxapahaw is Trivia Night! Most Wednesdays we will be there playing along with you. Prizes include a $25 gift certificate for the Eddy and a free dessert!
